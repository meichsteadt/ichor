Our Story

Ichor Studios is a small team, and we're more than just a Web Design and Development Agency. We prioritize close realtionships with who we work with more than anything else, so we can bring what's best for each client. We mainly work with small business and nonprofit organizations. We believe small businesses are the lifeblood of any community, and their websites are invaluable bridges that connect them to thier communities. We also believe in giving back, and offer a special discount to two nonprofit organizations each year.

To provide the best work, CEO Jennifer Qian makes sure each and every team member at Ichor loves their jobs and their lives. She trusts and respects the team and expects each team member to do the same with eachother; because having a strong bond amongst the team creates an enjoyable work environment and a high work efficency as well. Because she values each member's personal growth as well as the growth of Ichor, Jennifer encourages each member to try working in different departments, sometimes even multiple departments at the same time.
We are in the process of studio hunting in the Portland and Vancouver area, and in the future we look forward to grow as a global agency with multiple, small studios throughout the world.
